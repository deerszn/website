ECOTEK

primer

eco-
  1. prefix connecting to the environment or ecology.
tek
  1. acronym "traditional ecological knowledge": the ongoing accumulation of knowledge, practice, and belief, that is acquired by indigenous people over hundreds or thousands of years through direct contact with the environment.
  2. slang from "technique". term used by the psychonaut and drug communities to describe methods for growing/creating psychoactive substances.
