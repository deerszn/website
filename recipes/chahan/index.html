fried rice

ingredients

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 1 lb ground beef / protein of your choice (these instructions are specifically for ground beef)
  • 4 bell peppers, preferrably one of each color (green, red, orange, yellow)
  • 2 bunches of green onions
  • 8-12 eggs
  • butter
  • salt, pepper, & msg

you will also need a pan/wok, a spatula, and a large bowl/container.

instructions

  1. rinse the rice until the water runs clear. strain. (optional tbh, i kinda like it sticky)
  2. for every 1 cup of rice, use 1 cup of water. add to rice cooker. set it to cook while you do everything else.
  3. dice peppers & slice green onions
  4. put glob of butter in pan. set stovetop to medium-low so it starts melting (but not burning).
  5. once butter is melted, add ground beef. season to taste with salt, pepper, and msg.
  6. once browned, discard most of the grease and put the cooked beef into the big container.
  7. return pan to burner. add a little more butter, followed by diced peppers & onions. season them and sweat them for a while. when finished, add to big container.
  8. return pan to burner. add more butter. crack in eggs. scramble. season. when finished, add to big container.
  9. turn off burner. mix the big bowl with all the cooked ingredients. season.
  10. rice is probably done by now. add to big bowl. season. mix. taste, season again if necessary.
  11. scoop into individual bowl. eat.
